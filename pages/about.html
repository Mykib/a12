A little history ...

The Nissan A-series motor is found in Datsun and Nissan* vehicles from the 1960s to the early 2000s. These motors were developed by the Austin Motor Company based in Britain and further licensed and modified by Nissan to use in their vehicles. The A12 specifically, is a 1171cc variant found in the Nissan/Datsun Sunnys of the 1970s and is the motor that came with, and is currently fitted to my own personal Datsun Sunny.

*Datsun and Nissan are the same motor vehicle manufacturer. The name Datsun was chosen to appeal to the western market when Nissan was expanding their market out of Japan.
